Property Description

Purvanchal Royal Square, located in Sector CHI-V, Greater Noida, is a mixed-use development offering both retail spaces and residential units. The project features 72 retail stores and 154 fully furnished 1 BHK studio apartments, each designed to be well-ventilated and east or west-facing. Amenities include a deck pool, gym, and service floors on the 4th and 10th levels. The development is strategically located near educational hubs, healthcare facilities, and recreational spots like the Night Safari. Its proximity to major roads, Expo Mart, and the upcoming Jewar International Airport enhances its investment potential.
